TestiFaith Testimony Submission Guidelines

TestiFaith exists to glorify Jesus Christ and bear witness to God’s faithfulness. Every testimony shared on this platform should reflect that purpose.

1. Christ Must Be the Focus

All testimonies must clearly point to Jesus, not to an individual, ministry, brand, product, or personal achievement.

Share what God did, not how impressive you are

Give glory to God alone, not to human effort

Avoid language that centers on self elevation

“Let the redeemed of the Lord tell their story” — Psalm 107:2

2. Testimonies Are Not for Self Promotion

TestiFaith is not a marketing platform.

The following are not allowed:

Promotion of personal brands, businesses, ministries, or social media pages

Requests for followers, donations, or support

Mention of contact details, links, or handles

Using testimony as a tool to build influence or visibility

Your story should exalt God’s work, not build personal recognition.

3. Speak of God’s Faithfulness, Not Personal Glory

Testimonies should highlight:

God’s mercy, grace, power, healing, provision, or direction

His faithfulness through trials, waiting seasons, or breakthroughs

Transformation that points back to God’s hand, not personal strength

Avoid exaggeration or storytelling meant to impress.

4. Be Honest, Reverent, and Edifying

Share truthfully and with humility

Avoid sensationalism or dramatic exaggeration

Ensure your testimony encourages faith, hope, and trust in God

Testimonies should build up the body of Christ, not cause confusion or comparison.

5. Keep It Respectful and Biblically Aligned

Language must be respectful, reverent, and clean

Content should align with biblical truth

Avoid controversial doctrines, accusations, or divisive narratives

6. Protect Privacy and Dignity

Do not expose private details of others without consent

Avoid naming individuals, organizations, or institutions in a harmful way

Keep sensitive information wise and discreet

7. Testimonies Are Subject to Review

All testimonies submitted on TestiFaith:

Are reviewed before publishing

May be edited for clarity or alignment with platform values

May be declined if they do not meet these guidelines

This is done to protect the spiritual purpose of the platform.

8. The Heart Behind the Testimony Matters

Before submitting, ask yourself:

Does this testimony glorify Jesus?

Does it point people to faith in God?

Would God be pleased with the motive behind this submission?

If the answer is yes, you are welcome to share.
