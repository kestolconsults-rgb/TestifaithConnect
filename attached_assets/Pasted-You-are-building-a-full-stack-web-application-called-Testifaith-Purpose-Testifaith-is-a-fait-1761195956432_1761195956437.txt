You are building a full-stack web application called “Testifaith.”

Purpose:
Testifaith is a faith-based community platform for Christians and believers to share testimonies, read others’ stories, and strengthen their faith in God. The app will provide encouragement, hope, and inspiration through real-life experiences of God’s goodness.

Core Features:
1. **Homepage**
   - Welcoming banner with tagline: “Share Your Story. Strengthen Your Faith.”
   - Daily “Testimony of the Day” section highlighting one impactful story.
   - Navigation to major categories: Healing, Marriage, Fruitfulness, Finance, Breakthrough, Deliverance, and Others.

2. **Testimony Feed**
   - List or card view of testimonies shared by users.
   - Each testimony includes: title, category, author name (or “Anonymous”), date, and story content.
   - Option to “Amen,” “Encourage,” or comment.

3. **Post a Testimony**
   - Simple form where users can submit a new testimony with fields: title, category, and full story.
   - Option to post anonymously.

4. **Category Pages**
   - Dedicated pages for each category (Healing, Marriage, Fruitfulness, Finance, etc.).
   - Displays testimonies under that category.

5. **Testimony of the Day**
   - Automatically select or highlight one featured testimony daily.

6. **Encouragement Section**
   - Short daily verses or quotes on faith and hope.
   - Option for users to submit encouraging Bible verses.

7. **User Account (optional MVP feature)**
   - Simple authentication system (sign up, login, logout).
   - Users can manage their testimonies.

Design & Tech Stack:
- Use modern, clean UI with soft, peaceful colors (white, light gold, gentle blue tones).
- Framework: React (frontend), Node.js + Express (backend), MongoDB (database).
- Responsive design (mobile-friendly).
- Include header, footer, and navigation menu.
- Optional admin dashboard to manage testimonies and highlight “Testimony of the Day.”

App Goals:
- Build a safe, uplifting space for believers to share and read testimonies.
- Help users grow their faith through the experiences of others.
- Encourage gratitude and hope through shared faith journeys.

Branding:
- App Name: Testifaith
- Tagline: “Share Your Story. Strengthen Your Faith.”
- Logo idea: A glowing cross or open book with rays of light (can be added later).

Deliverable:
Generate the code for a working prototype of the Testifaith web app with the above structure and functionality. Include comments and clear folder organization for easy modification later.
